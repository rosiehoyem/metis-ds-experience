12 Weeks at Metis

A Data Science Bootcamp Experience by Rosie Hoyem

Image created using TensorFlow


I attended the Metis Data Science program in Chicago from January to April of 2017. I've collected a detailed account of that experience here. If you're thinking of applying to do a data science program like Metis, I hope you find this helpful in making your decision. If you're here because you might want to hire me, I hope this gives you some insight into how I think and what I learned during this experience.

I've included several "cheat sheet" sections on topics such as classification algorithms, clusting algorithms, and big data concepts. I also include a write-up of my final passion project and closing thoughts.
